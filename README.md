# Clear Cookies

A Playnite extension to clear cookies from user-input domain, 
so you don't have to clear all cookies and log out of every platform 
just to troubleshoot a login problem from a specific site.

# Usage

Click Playnite Icon > Extensions > Clear cookies.

Input the domain names you want to clear cookies on.

<img width="784" height="587" alt="clear steam cookie input box screenshot" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c9e203b3-285e-473f-a51c-646659bcfc34" />

Now you can start troubleshooting why your Steam (or whatever platform) login doesn't work
without sign out of every platform.
